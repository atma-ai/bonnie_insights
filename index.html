Frequently Asked Questions

When are the reports sent?

    The report is sent on the last business day of each calendar month. It includes an analysis of the reviews left on your Google Maps profile during the current month.

How many Google Maps reviews do you analyze?

We currently analyze the most recent 50 to 100 reviews for your report. We've found that this range strikes the perfect balance between providing actionable insights without overwhelming you with too much data.

Why are you offering this service?

We believe that all businesses—whether they're just starting out or well-established—should be able to easily learn from their Google Maps reviews. Our goal is to help you make better business decisions without needing to invest in expensive tools or complicated software.

Do I need to have technical knowledge to use this service?

Not at all! Our reports are easy to read and understand, designed for business owners and managers who want clear insights without any technical complexity. We handle the data analysis for you, so you can focus on making informed decisions.

How accurate are the insights provided by Bonnie Insights?

Bonnie Insights uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to analyze reviews with high accuracy. Our sentiment analysis and trend identification have been tested to achieve over 95% accuracy. However, we always encourage businesses to use our insights in conjunction with their own understanding of their customers and market.
